FAQ

Q: What printers can i use this firmware on?
 A: You can use this firmware on the Wanhao i3 Plus and clones/rebrands:
Wanhao Duplicator i3 Plus
Cocoon Create Touch
Monoprice Maker Select Plus (no way to restore original firmware!)

Q: What if i have problems with this firmware on my Maker Select Plus?
 A:Monoprice hasn't made their firmware public yet, you can't revert back to the original firmware.
You can however flash the official Wanhao or Cocoon Create firmware, if you have problems.
The Cocoon Create firmware should be nearly identical to the Monoprice firmware.
I've already contacted Monoprice multiple times, they just dont respond at all.
So if you would like to be able to reflash your original firmware, contact them to let them know the community has a demand for the firmware!

Q: Can this break my printer?
 A: Nope, extremely unlikely. You can always revert back to the original firmwares (except on the Monoprice Maker Select Plus).

Q: I found a bug. How can i report it?
A: You can either open an issue on the github page or send me an email: s...@gmail.com

Q: Why do my aborted prints count as completed prints?
A: Thats the way Marlin's statistics work. Only power outages or similar issues get counted as failed prints.

Q: Help, i cant flash the touchscreen firmware
A: In some cases microSD cards bigger then 8GB can cause issues. Try a smaller one!

Q: I know how to code. Can i contribute to this project?
A: Of course! Feel free to send pull requests. You can also contact me if you have technical questions! s...@gmail.com
